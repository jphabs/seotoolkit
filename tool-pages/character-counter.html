How to Use the Character Counter

Simply paste or type your text into the box below. The tool will calculate and display the total character count, both with and without spaces.

Why use this tool? This character counter is ideal for checking character limits on social media posts, SEO meta descriptions, blog posts, and more. It helps ensure your text fits within the required limits.
