Password Strength Checker

In today's digital age, creating a strong and secure password is essential for safeguarding your online accounts and personal data. Our Password Strength Checker helps you evaluate the strength of your password based on various factors such as length, character diversity, and complexity. It provides a quick and efficient way to ensure your password is robust enough to withstand potential security threats, reducing the likelihood of hacking attempts.

The tool analyzes your password and assigns it a strength rating, ranging from "Weak" to "Strong", based on criteria such as:

  • Password Length: Longer passwords are generally more secure. Our checker allows you to specify the minimum length for your password, ensuring a minimum threshold of security.
  • Character Diversity: The more diverse your password is, the harder it is to crack. Our tool checks if your password includes a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Pattern Recognition: We evaluate whether your password uses easily predictable patterns (like "12345" or "password"), which are less secure and vulnerable to brute-force attacks.
