Why is URL Encoding Important?

URL encoding plays a crucial role in ensuring that the web addresses you send and receive are properly formatted for browsers and web servers. It converts characters that have special meanings in URLs into a standardized format that can be safely transmitted. Without encoding, certain characters might cause errors or alter the intended URL.

How URL Decoding Helps with Web Development

Decoding URLs is equally important for web developers. It helps restore a URL to its original form, making it easier to process and display data. For instance, if you receive a URL with encoded characters, decoding it makes the information legible again, allowing you to work with clean data.
